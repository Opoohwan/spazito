How it works

Three steps, then it runs itself.

1

Subscribe

Enter your mobile number below and check the consent box. That's the whole signup — no account, no password, no app to install.

2

Get your daily text

3

Text back to customize

What you get

A deliberately small tool that does one thing well.

Automated daily alerts

Messages are generated automatically by the application on a fixed weekday schedule. You don't have to ask for them.

Your own watchlist

Track the S&P, gold, and silver out of the box — then add or remove any ticker you like, just by texting.

Pause anytime

Text pause to stop the alerts and resume to start them again. Text STOP to unsubscribe entirely.

Verifiable messages

Every alert carries a signed code so you can confirm it genuinely came from your service and wasn't altered or spoofed.

Frequently asked questions

When do the texts arrive?

Once per weekday, in the late afternoon (around 5pm Pacific). Nothing on weekends.

How much does it cost?

Spazito is free to receive. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

What can I track?

You start with the S&P 500, gold, and silver. Text add followed by any ticker symbol to track more, and remove to drop one.

How do I stop the messages?

Text pause to temporarily stop the daily alerts, or STOP to unsubscribe completely. Text HELP at any time for assistance.

Is this financial advice?

No. Spazito delivers publicly available price information for informational purposes only. It is not financial advice, and no guarantee is made as to accuracy or timeliness.

Will my number be shared?

Never. We do not sell, rent, or share subscriber phone numbers or opt-in information with anyone, for any purpose. See the Privacy Policy.
