Spazito — Automated Market Price Text Alerts

Spazito is an automated notification service. Its software sends a scheduled daily text message with stock and precious-metals prices (for example, "S&P 751 | Gold 382 | Silver 70.00") to users who subscribe below. Messages are generated automatically by the application on a weekday schedule. Subscribers can text commands (ADD, REMOVE, LIST, PAUSE, RESUME, HELP) to choose which tickers they receive and to pause or resume their alerts.
