Spazito — Daily Market Price Text Alerts

Spazito sends a daily text with stock and precious-metals prices (for example, "S&P 751 | Gold 382 | Silver 56.11") to people who sign up. Recipients can text commands back (ADD, REMOVE, LIST, PAUSE, RESUME, HELP) to manage their tickers and pause or resume alerts.
