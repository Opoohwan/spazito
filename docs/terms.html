The service

Spazito is an automated notification service that sends subscribers a daily text message containing publicly available market prices (stocks and precious metals). Messages are generated automatically by software on a fixed weekday schedule. Subscribers may text commands (ADD, REMOVE, LIST, PAUSE, RESUME, HELP) to manage which prices they receive.

Subscribing and consent

By submitting your mobile number through the sign-up form and checking the consent box, you agree to receive recurring automated text messages from Spazito at that number. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message frequency is approximately one message per weekday. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out

You may stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance. Texting pause temporarily suspends the daily alerts; resume restarts them.
