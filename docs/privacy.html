Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Spazito is an automated notification service that sends daily market-price text alerts to subscribers who opt in. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and — most importantly — what we never do with it.

Information we collect

We collect only your mobile phone number, submitted by you through the sign-up form on this site. We also retain the list of ticker symbols you choose to track and whether your alerts are paused, so the service can send you the right message.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, payment information, or any other personal data. There is no account and no password.

How we use it

Your phone number is used for exactly one purpose: to deliver the automated price-alert text messages you signed up for, and to process the commands you text back (such as adding or removing a ticker, or pausing alerts).

What we never do

We do not sell, rent, or share subscribers' mobile numbers, opt-in information, or any other personal information with third parties or affiliates for any purpose, including marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing. Your number is used only to operate this alert service.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency is approximately one message per weekday, plus replies to any commands you send. Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier's plan.

Opting out and getting help

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any text. Reply HELP for assistance. You can also text pause to temporarily suspend alerts and resume to restart them.

Data retention

We retain your phone number and watchlist only for as long as you remain subscribed. If you opt out, your information is no longer used to send messages.

Security

Subscriber information is stored in a secured configuration store and is not exposed publicly. Every outbound alert is cryptographically signed so recipients can verify that a message genuinely originated from the service and was not altered.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
